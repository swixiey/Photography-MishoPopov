About me

I'm a Seattle-based film photographer drawn to quiet, in-between moments — the soft light before a room fills up, the texture of a wall that's seen decades pass.

Film slows me down in the best way. Every frame costs something, which means every frame earns its place. I shoot mostly on the streets and in people's homes, always with available light.
